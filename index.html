Welcome to Wissowl

This blog is an experiment on caring through words. One beautiful word in caring is welcome. Welcome is much more than a hello or such other greetings. By saying welcome you do not only recognize somebody as human being and show peaceful intentions. Welcome is a blessing.

Welcome welcomes you as a guest of a physical or virtual home and invites to take a seat round the hearth. Welcome conjures up images of a warm broth in a stormy night, it moves you a step into an internal circle. It says love.
