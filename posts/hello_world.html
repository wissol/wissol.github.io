Hello, world

This, the first post in my blog, must be auspiciously called, Hello, World, and hence, here it is titled as such. I do not presume that many among the world will rush to read this rushed pamphlet, so I shall be brief. It is March, the 26th, 2016 and hereby I declare my blog began.

I intend to deal mostly about professional matters: HTLM5, CSS3, Sass, JS, Python and, my attempts at Haskell. Personal productivity, life design and minimalism, 'a la' Leo Babauta will also be there. Last but not least, I may also dwell on literature, science, solo-wargaming, solo-role-playing, conlanging, and all that sort of stuff, oh and religion. But no politics, not really, there's a place where a man must know where to stop.
